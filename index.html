Directions Ingredients
  1. Pound chicken breasts if they are too thick. Place cheese and ham slice on eac breast within 1/2 inch of edges. Fold the edges of the chicken over the filling and secure with toothpicks. Mix the flour and paprika in a small bowl, and coat the chicken pieces.

  2. Heat the butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat, and cook the chicken until browned on all sides. add the wine and bouillon. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 30 minutes, untill chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear.

  3. Remove the toothpicks and transfer the breasts to a warm platter. Blend the cornstarch with the cream in a small bowl, and whisk slowly into skillet. Cook, stirring until thickened, and pour over the chicken. Serve warm.
